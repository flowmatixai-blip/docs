### **What are Pre-Built Chatbot Templates?**

**Pre-Built Chatbot Templates** are ready-to-use message structures designed to help you quickly create WhatsApp messages for:

- **Broadcast campaigns**
- **Drip campaigns**

These templates save time by providing predefined formats that can be customized with your content, media, and buttons. They can be used as-is or modified to match your business needs.

### **How to Use Pre-Built Chatbot Templates**

#### **Step 1: Access Pre-Built Templates**

1. Go to your WhatsApp bot dashboard.
2. Click **Pre-Built Chatbot Templates \> Message Templates**.
3. Browse the **template gallery** to choose a template closest to your campaign needs.
4. Select the template to **customize** or create an entirely new template from scratch.

#### **Step 2: Create a Regular Template**

1. Enter a **Template Name** and choose a **Category**.
2. Select **Template Type \> Regular**.
3. (Optional) Add a **Header**: text, image, video, or document.
4. Enter the **Text Body**.
5. (Optional) Add a **Footer**.
6. Add a **Button** if needed:
   - Call-to-action button (website or call)
   - Quick reply button (redirect to chatbot)
   - Or select **None**
7. Save as **Draft** to edit later, or **Submit for Approval**.
8. Approved templates display a **green icon** and are available for campaigns.

#### **Step 3: Create a Carousel Template**

1. Enter a **Template Name** and choose a **Category**.
2. Select **Template Type \> Carousel**.
3. Add **Text Body** for the carousel.
4. Add **Cards** for each item:
   - Upload an **image** for each card.
   - Enter a **description**.
   - Add a **Button** (call-to-action or quick reply).
5. Save as **Draft** or **Submit for Approval**.
6. Once approved, carousel templates can be used for campaigns featuring multiple products or offers.
